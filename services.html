Blood Products

We provide hospitals with the following lifesaving blood products 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Platelets

  • Platelets pheresis, leukocyte-reduced
  • Platelets pheresis irradiated
  • Platelets pheresis, CMV-negative
  • Platelets pheresis, HLA matched
  • Platelets pheresis, HPA-1a (PLA1) N
  • Platelet additive solution (PAS) platelets
  • Pathogen reduced platelets (in PAS)

Red Blood Cells

  • Leukocyte-reduced
  • CMV negative
  • Deglycerolized
  • Washed
  • For pediatric use
  • Frozen
  • Antigen negative

Plasma

  • Plasma frozen within 24 hours (PF24)
  • 24 Hours Room Temperature (PF24RT24)
  • Fresh frozen plasma (FFP)
  • Plasma, cryoprecipitate reduced
  • Cryoprecipitated AHF
  • Cryoprecipitated AHF, pooled
  • Liquid plasma


Whole Blood

  • Leukocyte-reduced
  • Non-leukocyte reduced
  • CMV negative
  • Irradiated
  • For pediatric use


Blood for Research

  • Whole blood
  • Red blood cells
  • Platelets
  • Source leukocytes
  • Mononuclear cells
